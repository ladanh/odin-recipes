Strawberry Strudle Recipe

How to Make a Strawberry Strudle

Description

This morning I woke up craving a classic strawberry strudle with filling. These soft and tasty pastries make for a perfect breakfast snack.

You can fill these with any flavor of preserves you like such as apricot, blueberries, or raspberries.

Ingredients

  • Pastry: store bought is best
  • Preserves: high quality and thick
  • Eggs: for an eggwash

Steps

  1. Make egg wash - keep this aside for now.
  2. Cut pastry sheets into small rectangle strips.
  3. Add preserves in the center of the pastry strip.
  4. Fold pastry and press to seal. Then, flip onto a cookie tray, lined with parchment paper.
  5. Slice tops so that steam can easily escape.
  6. Brush egg wash on top and sprinkle some sanding sugar
  7. Bake - until the strudels are puffy and golden brown. Enjoy!
